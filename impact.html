IMPACTS

For seven decades, Adhinanda Foundation has tirelessly dedicated itself to combatting poverty and social exclusion in India. Through meticulously designed and expansive initiatives, the foundation addresses critical areas such as health, education, livelihoods, and disaster preparedness and response. By providing holistic support and interventions, Adhinanda Foundation aims to uplift marginalized communities, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and achieve sustainable livelihoods. As per more than 100 nations worldwide, Adhinanda Foundation is part of a global movement striving for a world where every individual can live with dignity and security. Through collaboration, innovation, and unwavering commitment, the foundation continues to make significant strides towards its vision, leaving an enduring impact on the lives of those it serves across India.

Sustainable Development Goals

Longevity

Over seven decades of operation, Adhinanda Foundation has built a legacy of meaningful impact, establishing itself as a reliable force for positive change in the lives of countless individuals.

Focus on Alleviating Poverty & Social Exclusion

Through its dedicated efforts in poverty alleviation & social inclusion, the foundation actively tackles the root causes of inequality.

Comprehensive Programmes

Adhinanda Foundation's holistic approach addresses the multifaceted challenges faced by vulnerable populations, offering tailored solutions in crucial areas such as healthcare, education etc.

Global Reach

As an integral part of the CARE International Confederation, spanning operations across more than 100 countries, Adhinanda Foundation contributes to a widespread movement.

Empowerment

Through strategic interventions in education, healthcare, and livelihood support, the foundation empowers communities to take charge of their futures, fostering self-sufficiency.

Collaboration and Volunteerism

By actively engaging grassroots volunteers, Adhinanda Foundation cultivates a culture of collaboration and collective action, harnessing the diverse skills.

Tangible Impact

Supporters and volunteers witness firsthand the tangible outcomes of their contributions, whether through improved access to education, healthcare services.

Opportunity for Engagement

Adhinanda Foundation provides individuals with a meaningful platform to engage, learn, and contribute to social change efforts.
