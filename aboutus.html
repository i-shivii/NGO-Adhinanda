About Us

Empowering women and girls holds the key to lifting entire families and communities out of poverty, catalyzing positive change across generations.

As a non-profit entity, our mission is centered on enhancing the capabilities of communities to promote the empowerment of marginalized women and girls. Through our sustainable and comprehensive initiatives in Healthcare, Economic Empowerment, Education, and Disaster Response & Resilience, we offer innovative strategies to tackle entrenched development challenges. Leveraging both global expertise through international partnerships and our internal reservoir of knowledge, we collaborate with a diverse network of stakeholders to deliver impactful outcomes on a large scale.

Our aim is to bring lasting impact at scale by:

  • Working with individuals and communities to raise consciousness, self-esteem, confidence, and aspirations to change their world, and provide them the knowledge, skills, and capabilities to do so.

  • Changing relationships to address the inequality that persists in intimate relations, family and social networks, marketplaces, and community or citizen groups. We strive to heal and activate these networks.

  • Strengthening systems to increase the social investment by governments which offers us the opportunity to work closely with systems and multiply impact at scale.

Complex, deep rooted issues need Strong Partnership-Corporates, Government and NGOs. Let us join hands to being positive change to marginalized communication.

Agricultural Field

Adhinanda revolutionizes agriculture by introducing novel farming methods, providing essential resources, and promoting sustainable techniques. By empowering farmers, they enhance resilience and prosperity in rural areas, turning barren fields into vibrant centers of productivity and growth. Through innovative approaches, Adhinanda cultivates thriving agricultural communities, ensuring sustainable development and economic prosperity for generations to come.

Girls from underserved demographics

Adhinanda passionately champions girls' rights, emphasizing access to education, healthcare, and protection from violence. By empowering girls, she believes societies unlock their full potential, fostering equity and prosperity. She advocates for policies and initiatives that ensure every girl can thrive, contributing to a more inclusive and dynamic society. Adhinanda's dedication to this cause reflects a vision of a world where every girl can fulfill her dreams and aspirations, shaping a brighter future for all.

Education

The Adhinanda Foundation is dedicated to advancing children's education by guaranteeing access to high-quality learning experiences for all. Through its pioneering initiatives and active involvement within communities, the foundation empowers the forthcoming generation, laying the groundwork for a more promising future. By fostering innovation and fostering community engagement, Adhinanda Foundation strives to create a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Enhancing the agency of females

Our mission is to empower marginalized women and girls, fostering sustainable positive changes in their lives and livelihoods. We recognize the vital role of corporate involvement in achieving this vision. Leveraging our expertise in designing sustainable solution-based models, we align closely with your CSR objectives. Together, we can create meaningful impact by unlocking opportunities and enhancing the well-being of women and girls in marginalized communities.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We collaborate closely with partners to address their pressing challenges by devising, executing, and overseeing sustainable and scalable initiatives. Our strategic alliances encompass a comprehensive, long-range strategy spanning key sectors like Health, Education, Livelihood, Humanitarian Response, Women Empowerment, and Gender Equality. Through this integrated approach, we strive to understand, design, implement, monitor, and evaluate programs that make a lasting impact and foster positive change.

Employee Engagement

Our employees drive lasting change for marginalized women and girls by fundraising and volunteering. They choose causes close to them, supporting them through payroll giving and volunteer work. This initiative empowers our team to create meaningful impacts, fostering equity and positive change for women and girls in marginalized communities. Through their efforts, they uplift and support those in need, promoting a more inclusive and equitable society..

Volunteering

Join Adhinanda Foundation as a grassroots volunteer, immersing yourself in diverse projects spanning from disaster relief efforts to underserved communities. Whether you're supporting education, healthcare, or livelihoods, your involvement yields lasting impact. Embrace the chance to learn and contribute, making a tangible difference where it's most needed. Together, we foster positive change, empowering communities toward a brighter, more equitable future. Your dedication strengthens our collective resolve to address pressing societal challenges.
